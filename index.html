About Me

Full-Stack Software Engineer

Hello! I'm Royce Calvin, a passionate Full-Stack Software Engineer with a deep love for solving complex problems with technology. I specialize in building dynamic, user-friendly web applications using a mix of JavaScript, Python, and various modern frameworks like React, Redux, and Express. I enjoy working on both the frontend and backend, always learning and growing in my craft.

My journey into software development began with a fascination for how things work behind the scenes. From learning game development at Full Sail University to diving into web applications at App Academy, I've had the opportunity to explore and work with a wide range of technologies.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me listening to music (especially Jessie Murph and The Story So Far), spending time with family, or building side projects to further hone my skills. I'm always looking to collaborate on new, exciting projects and to continue evolving as a developer.

Feel free to reach out to me if you'd like to chat about software development, technology, or just want to connect!
